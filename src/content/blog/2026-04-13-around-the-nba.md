## LBJ's Insane Assist Streak
LeBron James just keeps on trucking. The King became the first non-point guard to reach 12,000 assists. That's ridiculous. **HoosierDaddyKid** [shares](https://x.com/mcten/status/2042831418414502037) the news, and we've got to give it up for LBJ's incredible all-around game.

> "LBJ becomes the first non-point guard to reach 12,000 assists." - **HoosierDaddyKid**

## MVP Chatter and Playoff Contenders
The MVP conversation is heating up, and Jaylen Brown is making a strong case. **HoosierDaddyKid** [reports](https://x.com/LevAkabas/status/2042641877002764643) that Brown is on track to finish 5th in MVP voting. Meanwhile, the Pacers are tanking hard, securing a bottom-three record and potentially the best lottery odds.

> "Jaylen Brown is on track to finish 5th in MVP voting." - **HoosierDaddyKid**

## Historic Triple-Doubles
The NBA saw its first game with three players recording a triple-double. **HoosierDaddyKid** [shares](https://x.com/realapp/status/2042812888574804218) the impressive feat. Which player will be the next to achieve this?

## Lottery Loot and Spurs' What-Ifs
The Pacers' tanking strategy might pay off with a top pick. **loki** [laments](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=390) that the Pacers' potential win could mean another star for a team that already went to the Finals.

> "I hate that they're making me root for the Clippers. Fingers crossed that pick falls to #5." - **loki**

The Spurs, on the other hand, are probably kicking themselves for not landing the #1 pick. **RasheedsBigWhiteSpot** [paints](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=171) a scary picture of what could've been:

> "Can you imagine if the Spurs had landed #1??? They could've sported a lineup of Fox, Castle, Flagg and Wemby. They could've played your 85 year old Aunt Sylvia at SF and still won 10 straight championships."

## Mavs Fans Weigh In

## Playoff Predictions and Concerns
The playoffs are around the corner, and teams are jockeying for position. **Chicagojk** [worries](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404) about the Hawks' Rischacher, a #1 pick who's been underwhelming:

> "I am pretty sure if AD did not get injured the KP, Rishacher, first round pick would have been on the table."

**FireNicoHarrison** [weighs in](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=4633) on the Kuminga vs. Risacher debate:

## A Wild West and Mavs' Future
