The Dallas Mavericks fan community has been buzzing this week, and we're here to bring you the highlights. From the ongoing search for a new General Manager to the team's tanking strategy, we've got you covered.

### Next GM Watch

The search for the next General Manager has been a hot topic of discussion, with many fans weighing in on potential candidates. [F Gump](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=412) kicked off the conversation by sharing an article from the Dallas Morning News, which mentioned Chicago Bulls executive Masayoshi Soken and former Houston Rockets GM Daryl Morey as potential targets. Notably, Morey was the second name on the short list, and the article emphasized the need for an expert with a strong track record.

[F Gump](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=412) believes that the Mavs need someone with deep expertise and a willingness to spend to attract top talent. "High-level EXPERT execs at the GM level do get hired away," he noted. "Money talks, when the owner is willing to spend." [cow](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=92) chimed in, jokingly suggesting that it was "our idea" all along.

### AROUND the NBA:

Some exciting developments around the league caught the attention of Mavs fans. [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) shared some impressive stats, including LeBron James becoming the first non-point guard to reach 12,000 assists. Additionally, Jaylen Brown's MVP chances and the Pacers' odds of landing the number one pick in the lottery were also discussed.

### MAVS NEWS:

The Mavs have been struggling this season, but some players have shown promise. [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404) highlighted Max's three-point shooting, noting that he needs to make at least 6 out of 14 threes in the final two games to finish the season over 40% from beyond the arc. Max is currently fourth in the league in free throw percentage, and [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404) believes that his game will adapt well to a more talented roster.

### Trade & FA 2025-26: Free Agency Starts 5pm CST/6pm EST

As the offseason approaches, fans are eager to see what moves the Mavs will make. While there's still much speculation, one thing is certain: the team will have a lot of cap space to work with.
