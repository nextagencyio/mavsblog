## Record-Breaking Feats 
The NBA season has ended, and as expected, there are plenty of records being broken. [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) shared some impressive stats on the MavsBoard forum. Kon Knueppel and LaMelo Ball became the 2nd duo in NBA history to finish 1st and 2nd in made 3's in a season, behind Steph Curry and Klay Thompson. 

> "Kon Knueppel and LaMelo Ball became the 2nd duo in NBA history to finish 1st and 2nd in made 3's in a season behind Steph Curry and Klay Thompson." - [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159)

Only 18 players played the full 82-game schedule, which is a remarkable feat in itself. Nikola Jokic made history again, and [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) shared the excitement.
