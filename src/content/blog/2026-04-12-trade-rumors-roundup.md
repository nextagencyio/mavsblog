The Dallas Mavericks are no strangers to trade rumors, and with the offseason approaching, the MavsBoard community is buzzing with potential deals. As we dive into the discussion threads, it's clear that fans are divided on what the team should do with some of its key players. Let's break down the latest trade proposals and insights from the community.

### Gafford on the Block?

Daniel Gafford has been a topic of discussion among Mavs fans, with some questioning his long-term fit with the team. [RoyTarpleysGhost](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=881) proposes trading Gafford for a 2026 1st-round pick, citing teams like the Lakers, Raptors, Hawks, and Bulls as potential suitors for a center. Others, like [loki](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=390), view Gafford as replaceable, suggesting that the team could explore cheaper options in free agency or target players like Goga.

However, not everyone is ready to give up on Gafford. [mvossman](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=482) notes that Gafford's health concerns and struggles this season may be due to the lack of an elite pick-and-roll partner. He argues that with the right fit, Gafford could still be a valuable asset to the team.

### Ivey and Risacher: Potential Targets?

The community is also discussing potential trades involving Jaden Ivey and Zaccharie Risacher. [mvossman](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=482) expresses skepticism about Ivey's potential, citing his injury history and limited development. On the other hand, [RoyTarpleysGhost](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=881) is hesitant to trade for Ivey or Risacher, with the latter being a former 1st overall pick who has yet to live up to expectations.

[RasheedsBigWhiteSpot](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=171) is blunt in his assessment of Risacher, stating that the player's peak may be a player like Nic Batum. He also questions Ivey's potential, labeling him a bust. However, [loki](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=390) suggests that Ron Holland, a young defensive standout, could be a target in a PJ Washington trade.

### Other Potential Moves

As the community discusses potential trades, other players are being mentioned as potential targets or assets to move. [mvossman](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=482) mentions players like Moody or Eason as potential targets, while [Scott41theMavs](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=97) discusses the importance of developing a pick-and-roll game to unlock the team's potential.

### Insights and Takeaways

As we sift through the discussion threads, a few key insights emerge:

* The community is divided on Gafford's long-term value, with some viewing him as replaceable and others seeing potential for growth.
* Ivey and Risacher are not universally viewed as valuable assets, with some fans questioning their potential or labeling them as busts.
* The team may need to explore cheaper options in free agency or target young players with upside.

Ultimately, the Mavericks' front office will have to make tough decisions this offseason. As the community continues to discuss and debate potential trades, one thing is clear: Mavs fans are passionate and invested in their team's success.

The conversation will undoubtedly continue on MavsBoard, where fans can share their thoughts and insights on the team's future. For now, it's clear that the community is eager to see what moves the front office will make to shape the team's roster for the upcoming season.
