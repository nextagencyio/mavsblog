## Lively's Injury and Future

The Mavs community is buzzing about [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404)'s update on Dereck Lively II's injury. According to Jason Kidd, Lively is still on crutches and in a boot, leaving his status for training camp uncertain:

> "I don't know when or what to expect D-Live right now. He's off the crutches. Hopefully soon we'll be in two shoes. I know that sounds kinda funny, but he's still in a boot...And then I think having that process of getting on the court, and hopefully he's ready for training camp, but medically I can't give you the answer today because I don't know." - [Unknown](https://www.mavsboard.com)

The injury has significant implications for Lively's future with the Mavs. [dirkfansince1998](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=14) thinks this could lead to a cheap extension: "Lively's injury could also create a unique opportunity for a cheap extension. Prior to his injury, I expected him to land in the ~30m range. That's obviously not the case right now."

## Kidd's Coaching and Medical Staff

[Mavs2021](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=504) is calling for a new GM and President of Basketball Operations to evaluate Lively properly and make a decision on a favorable extension. The community is also questioning Kidd's coaching and the team's medical staff. [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404) shares a post about a former Patriot player not being a fan of ex-Mavs director of health.

## Draft Disasters

The Mavs' draft decisions are also under scrutiny. [Mavs2021](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=504) thinks the team made a mistake by not taking a better prospect: "If the Mavs turned down the reported Atlanta deals around the Risacher + Cavs pick in favour of the Washington deal, Riccardi deserves to be the Mavs GM. Picked turned to crap and Risacher is French Josh Green."

[dirkfansince1998](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=14) has a more nuanced view of Risacher: "And I would still take him if all it takes is matching salary. Terrible 1st overall pick. Not necessarily a bad NBA player if he isn't asked to do things that you expect from a top pick."

## Contract Concerns

The community is discussing potential contract clauses for Lively, with [DallasMaverick](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=352) suggesting a Zion-like contract: "What have you done for me lately?... Maybe a Zion-like contract that’s predicated on games played?"

[F Gump](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=412) is skeptical about Lively accepting a low contract: "I doubt it. Any number low enough for the Mavs to consider will be wayyyyyyyyy too low for Lively to accept. Even a contract in 2027 will be tough to agree on."

## Conclusion

The Mavs community is eagerly awaiting Lively's return and a resolution to his contract situation. With the team's history of injuries and coaching decisions, there's a lot of uncertainty surrounding the franchise. As [michaeltex](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=23) puts it: "Well, that's depressing." The Mavs need to get their medical staff and coaching in order to ensure a healthy and successful future for players like Lively. Until then, fans will be on edge, wondering what the future holds for their beloved team.
