This perspective is understandable, given Irving's injury and the team's limited assets. "Let him get healthy, play next to Kyrie and Flagg, and rehab some trade value next year," he suggested. This approach would allow Irving to mentor Flagg and contribute to the team's growth, even if he's not the same player he once was.

This could mean that the team might trade Irving to acquire more draft assets or a player who fits their needs.
