## AROUND THE NBA: PLAYOFFS HEATING UP
The NBA playoffs are in full swing, and Mavs fans are keeping a close eye on the action. 

## WOLVES PACK A PUNCH
The Minnesota Timberwolves just took down the Denver Nuggets 113-96, with Nikola Jokic having a game to forget: 7-26 from the field, 2-10 from 3. As [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) noted, "Wolves blow out the Nuggets, 113-96. Joker has one of the worst games I can remember from him." 

> "Gobert harassed Joker into a bad shooting night. He's still a factor on defense, and he may still be miffed he finished 4th behind Wemby, Chet, and Ausar Thompson." - [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159)

## HAWKS FLYING HIGH
The Atlanta Hawks are making a statement, taking a 2-1 lead over the Knicks behind CJ McCollum's game-winning shot. [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404) thinks the Hawks are for real: "Hawks had an excellent trade deadline... I expect the KNicks to win a tough series still, but the Hawks are definitely stating their case."

> "I laugh when I hear the Mavs can't have any older players when rebuilding and then turning on the tv and seeing 34 year old McCollum make the game winning basket. In today's basketball, you don't need a full team of 22 year old if your are rebuilding." - [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404)

## NUGGETS' REBUILDING Woes
The Nuggets are paying for some questionable roster building decisions, according to [dirkfansince1998](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=14). "Basically downgraded every single year since they won the championship... MPJ/Cam Johnson trade was a disaster. Braun contract was a big mistake."

> "Teams losing contributors after a championship run is pretty normal but Nuggets (and Bucks, similar story) messed up big time." - [dirkfansince1998](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=14)

## KD RUINS ANOTHER TEAM?
The Brooklyn Nets aren't in the playoffs, but Kevin Durant's impact is still being felt. [Mavs2021](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=504) had a simple take: "Oh look KD ruined another team."
