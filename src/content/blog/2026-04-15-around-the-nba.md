## Dirty Play Alert

The Hornets' playoff win over the Heat was a nail-biter, but [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) wasn't thrilled about LaMelo Ball's questionable move on Bam Adebayo. 
> "LaMelo grabbed Bam's ankle while he was falling out of bounds. Bam hurt his back, and didn't return. Ball should have been called for a flagrant if you ask me. Borderline dirty play."

This play sparked a heated debate among fans, with some calling it a dirty move and others saying it was just a clever play. What do you think, Mavs fans? Was LaMelo's move a flagrant foul or just a desperate play?

## ROY Debate

The Rookie of the Year (ROY) award is always a hot topic, and [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) thinks Victor Wembanyama's poor shooting performance won't hurt his chances:
> "The ROY is a regular season award. Playoffs and Play-ins don't count. So Kon's poor shooting performance won't matter."

Meanwhile, [RasheedsBigWhiteSpot](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=171) isn't buying into the hype around LaMelo Ball:
> "Acting like Kon was anything other than maaaaaaaybe the 5th factor in Charlotte's turn around is a narrative basketball myth in the making."

Who do you think deserves the ROY award? Let us know in the comments!

## Mavs Fans React

[Mavs fan](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404) [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404) is feeling nostalgic for the good old days:
> "Fun game last night. I know it is sort of playoffs, but man the Mavs had no games like that this year. You could feel the tension. I miss those type of games."

We're all missing those intense games, [Chicagojk](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=404)! Hopefully, next season will bring more excitement.
