## Playoff Shenanigans
The playoffs are in full swing, and MavsBoard users are buzzing about the latest developments. [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) kicked off the discussion with a scathing take on the Orlando Magic: "Orlando ain't sh!t They need to blow that joint to smithereens!" 

> "Once the play became an ESPN thing, I would wager that 30 of 30 teams would get the same outcome." - [F Gump](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=412)

This sentiment was echoed by many, who felt that the Magic's demise was a long time coming. Meanwhile, [cow](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=92) was thrilled to see Andre Drummond hit a dagger three: "Andre Drummond dagger three was not on my bingo card." 

## Tanking and the Lottery
The Mavs' own struggles in the lottery have users reeling. [michaeltex](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=23) lamented: "I kind of hate that we lost out on at least tying for the #6 pick, pre-lottery. Even more so since MEM was playing a bunch of end-of-bench guys, 2 ways and 10 day contract guys." 

> "If karma is real, then DAL gets to jump over them into the top 3." - [michaeltex](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=23)

[Mavs fans are keeping their fingers crossed that the team will make a move in the top 3. 

## ROY Debate
The Rookie of the Year (ROY) discussion is heating up, with [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) weighing in: "Cooper should win it, but it will still be a close vote. The ROY is a regular season award. Playoffs and Play-ins don't count. So Kon's poor shooting performance won't matter." 

> "Acting like Kon was anything other than maaaaaaaybe the 5th factor in Charlotte's turn around is a narrative basketball myth in the making." - [RasheedsBigWhiteSpot](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=171)

## Mavs News and Notes
The Mavs' own rebuild is well underway, with [Scott41theMavs](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=97) offering some sage advice: "The old NBA adage... the alpha guy is the hardest piece to get. The Magic probably thought they had that with Banchero or Franz. Nope." 

> "The Mavs have an extremely difficult team building road ahead of them with no pick of their own '27-'30. But at least they have the bedrock to work with." - [Scott41theMavs](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=97)

As the team looks to the future, fans are excited to see what the front office will do next. Will they make a splashy trade, or play it safe in the draft? Only time will tell.

## Playoff Predictions
As the playoffs continue to unfold, users are making their predictions. [HoosierDaddyKid](https://www.mavsboard.com/member.php?action=profile&uid=159) thinks the Hornets have a chance: "The Hornets survive. We'll see how they fare in the next game." 

One thing's for sure: it's going to be a wild ride. So, who's your money on? The Blazers, led by Deni Avdija, or someone else entirely? 

The MavsBoard community is buzzing with excitement - and we've got our eyes on the prize. **The West is wide open, and Dallas is ready to pounce.**
